## What's in the pipeline for Pashto DAO?

Pashto DAO is a Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO). We aim to build an autonomous, transparent,
and inclusive community for the adoption of the Pashto language. Each community member will have a say in
the governorship without centralized leadership.
